How to Use Elder's GA Guide

A reference tool for ruling and teaching elders preparing for the General Assembly

Getting Around

📜 Browse

Your main library. All GA Minutes 1973–present, overtures, and study committee reports. Tap any document to open it. Use the tabs at the top to filter by type — Overtures, Minutes, Studies, or your Bookmarks.

🔍 Search

Full-text search across all 31,000+ document sections. Type any word, phrase, BCO reference (e.g. "BCO 20-4"), or commissioner name. Use the Year and Topic dropdowns to narrow results further.

📋 Docket

Your personal list of documents to track during the Assembly. Open any document and tap "+ Docket" to add it. Access your docket here for quick reference on the floor.

Reading Documents

Full Document View

Tap any document in Browse or Search to open it. The full text is stitched together into one continuous scrollable page — no pagination, no Next buttons.

🔖 Bookmarks

Tap the bookmark icon in the document toolbar to save any document. Bookmarked documents appear in the Bookmarks tab in Browse for quick access.

Personal Notes

Every document has a notes field at the bottom. Type anything — your notes are saved automatically to your device and persist between sessions.

BCO and Topic Tags

Documents are automatically tagged with the BCO chapters they reference and topic categories like worship, discipline, ordination, and missions. These tags appear in the document header and in search results.

Tips for GA Prep

Search by BCO section: Type "BCO 20" to find every document in the corpus that references Chapter 20 of the BCO.

Find overture history: Search the overture topic (e.g. "creation" or "women deacons") to see how the Assembly has handled similar overtures in prior years.

Build your docket before GA: Add every overture you need to vote on to your Docket. On the floor, open the Docket for one-tap access to any item.

Offline use: Once loaded, this app works without an internet connection. Load it on your phone before traveling to the Assembly city.

Install to home screen: In Safari, tap the Share button and choose "Add to Home Screen." The app opens full-screen like a native app.

What's in the Corpus

GA Minutes (1973–present)

Full text of all General Assembly minutes extracted from PDFs at pcahistory.org. Includes overtures, committee reports, recommendations, and actions taken.

Overtures (2019–2026)

Structured overture text from pcaga.org and pcaac.org for recent assemblies.

Study Committee Reports

Major study committee reports including the Creation Study Committee and Divorce and Remarriage report.
