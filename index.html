Ruling and Teaching Elders
Helper to the General Assembly

A reference tool for commissioners preparing for the General Assembly. Search and browse the full record of Assembly proceedings from 1973 to the present, alongside the Westminster Standards and constitutional documents.

Disclaimer

This application is an independent reference tool created to assist ruling and teaching elders in preparing for the General Assembly. It is not sanctioned, endorsed, or affiliated with the General Assembly, the Administrative Committee, or any official body of the Assembly. All source documents are publicly available. Users should verify any information against official sources before relying on it for Assembly business.
